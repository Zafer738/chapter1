Levian chocolate chip cookie recipe

Levain chocolate chip cookies—thick, gooey, and irresistibly rich, just like the original New York classic!

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups (230g) cake flour
  • 2 cups (275g) all-purpose flour
  • 1.5 tsp (5g) kosher salt
  • 2 tsp (8g) corn starch
  • 1/2 tsp (6g) baking soda
  • 1.25 cups (280g) unsalted butter
  • 1.25 cups (285g) brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (115g) white granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 2 cups (165g) walnuts
  • 1 lb (450g) chopped chocolate

Method

  1. Melt the unsalted butter in a saucepan and set aside to cool.
  2. Roughly chop the dark chocolate.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together cake flour, all-purpose flour, salt, cornstarch, and baking soda.
  4. In a stand mixer, whisk brown sugar and granulated sugar, then slowly add melted butter.
  5. Add eggs one at a time, followed by egg yolks.
  6. Switch to the paddle attachment and mix in the flour mixture until smooth.
  7. Add walnuts and chopped chocolate, mix until combined.
  8. Refrigerate the dough for at least 45 minutes.
  9. Weigh each dough ball to 6oz and place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  10. Bake at 425°F for 10-13 minutes.
  11. Cool on a wire rack before serving.
